Structural Imaging

Analyzing the brain's anatomy with high-precision MRI and CT scans. Structural imaging provides critical insights into brain morphology, helping diagnose conditions such as tumors, strokes, and degenerative diseases.

Functional Imaging

Investigating neural activity using PET and fMRI techniques. Functional imaging maps brain activity, showing how different regions respond to stimuli, which is crucial for understanding neurological disorders and cognitive functions.

Fused Brain Image

Integrating both imaging methods for enhanced medical diagnosis. Fusion of structural and functional imaging enhances the accuracy of disease detection, surgical planning, and neuroscience research.
